Welcome to Brugg Chamber of Commerce

About Brugg

Located in the heart of Aargau, Brugg is a charming city. With a rich history dating back to Roman times, Brugg knows how to blend the medieval charm with the modern world. Close to major cities such Zurich and Basel, the city is a perfect place to visit, live, and for business looking to thrive.

News in Brugg

With the festive season of Carnival approaching, Brugg is full with excitement and anticipation. This year, the Brugg Chamber of Commerce is planning diverse initiatives to ensure that businesses flourish and expand during this vibrant time of celebration. In collaboration with local artisans and entrepreneurs, the chamber is organizing a series of themed pop-up markets throughout the city center. This will allow your business to showcase unique crafts, culinary delights, and entertainment to captivate visitors and locals.
