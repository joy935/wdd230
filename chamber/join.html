Non-Profit Membership

Ideal for charitable organizations, the Non-Profit Membership offers essential benefits like member resources and networking opportunities at a budget-friendly price. Members gain visibility through website listings and enjoy discounted event participation and advertising options.

Free of Charge

Bronze Membership

Tailored for small businesses, the Bronze Membership provides enhanced visibility on the organization's website and substantial discounts on workshops and events. Members receive occasional spotlight features and access to exclusive networking events.

Cost: 250CHF/year

Silver Membership

Designed for businesses seeking moderate exposure, the Silver Membership offers prominent website placement, event discounts, and regular spotlight positions. Members also benefit from specialized training sessions tailored to their needs.

Cost: 500CHF/year

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership provides maximum exposure and exclusive benefits for businesses. Members enjoy premium website placement, VIP event invitations, advertising dominance, and personalized services like dedicated account management.

Cost: 1000CHF/year
